The average man or woman does not know how to manage a state, have the true cognitive power to make the best decisions for an entire society, hell even for local stability and the such. Unreliable Princedom of the Zoras and that Goron brotherhood truly left this realm weak for the Twili to simply seize. I believe that if there was more authority, more of a true presence of the Hylian Guard across the entire country, the invasion of the Twilight could at the very least be stalled whilst the Princessdom prepared for the scenario at hand.

I propose a question to you, Inogo... Should a force like the Twilight ever decide to march upon your realm and attempt their coup, would your disunified, lax lands, people and military truly be able to stop it?
Nevertheless, let me continue. It is good to see that you have been blessed by your deities with powers the commoners would not have, it is surely a good indicator that you are the right person to rule by the confirmation of the divines. Your policies of the right of the peoples, sufferage and anti-corruption is very much worthy of laud, but I hope that your pursuit for true democracy shall not lead to a weakness that can be exploited, as discussed above...

Overall, I respect Inogo Estima for who he is, however I would not be all that surprised if his pursuits leads to the weakening of the state's national security, something that could be exploited by foreign intervention.
I would suggest that you rethink your ideas of the rule of the people, as that can indeed bring instability as... Naturally, many people are simply too dull to be trusted, however I am not sure if you will... Either way, should we ever meet I would happily talk with oneself, and perhaps you can show me your skill of riding on the saddle? If it compares at all to the Hero of Courage, then I would certainly be impressed."
