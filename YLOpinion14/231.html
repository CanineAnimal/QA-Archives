"Oh, hello again! I must admit to some admiration of your work ethic at the very least good sir. You most assuredly put much effort into getting things done. I do have to note however that your oft-repeated commentary on our purported puppetry strikes me as a bit hypocritical. We all have our strings, attempting to pull us hither and yon, holding us down as much as they pick us up. It is how you respond to these strings that truly makes one a great person. Will you strain against those things that act against you, or will you allow those strings to dictate your actions as they are wont to do? Oft I see such conservatively oriented minds bent to following those external forces with astonishingly fanatical zeal. It is sufficient to make one wonder if there is some form of... defense mechanism involved. You seem very focused on maintaining the grip of tradition, sir! I cannot fault you for a great deal of this. After all, what is history if not a learning experience? It would seem however that you and I take vastly different lessons from history. 'Tis a shame." -Kaz'Ramael
