Huh, it seems that the emperor made his own air conditioner during a hot week - it seems that he has some skills with mechanics. While he is a monarch, he seems to be a pretty resourceful one, and who actually rules country to an extent.

Francis Ferdinand? I think that I recognize that name! Wasn't he the guy which was murdered by a Serbian nationalist named Gavrilo Princip? Yeah, his early marriage would be even more of a controversy nowadays, because I don't think that a 16 year old should marry, even if it is more of a political marriage. That said, while death of his parents came unexpected, it seems that he was made to inherit a conflict and properly respond to this, considering how quickly he realized what he had to do, and wasn't lax about it as well.

Him being a militarist in personality is nothing surprising - he is also a commander-in-chief of the Imperial army, and he is not someone who takes war very lightly. At least he doesn't spend his country's funds and resources on those wunderwaffe like nazis did - oversized, grand projects, yet useless in practice, and the resources could have gone for more basic tanks instead. He at least is honest with his conservatism, and him being pro-racial equality would place him as somewhat progressive even for modern day, but in his time and context I can imagine that it would definitely draw attention. He also seems to be a loving husband, while also acknowledging the differences, and not being an abusive drunkard.

I understand his hatred towards socialism and communists, despite our disagreements on that matter. I guess that it is also because we come from different settings, and I could actually manage to put commies in government without backfiring. At least he has got a welfare system to calm the workers down, and not laissez-faire claptrap which would accelerate discontent. He seems to be quite religious and conservative as well, which is good - traditions should be maintained. Also, him being an ''old soul'' is not that bad at all, considering that he acts more mature than a typical man of his age would. Perhaps it means that his empire still has hope to keep both ancaps and ''libertarian'' socialists at bay.
