Huh, an immortal lord-protector who is seemingly ageless, a gangster, and someone who is purge happy? I get vibes of a certain Immortal Tsar, except that he was just a face like Father America was, and in fact was completely mortal, rumored to have died in a hunting accident in 80s. Either way, I have a some sort of ''enemy's respect'' towards Russians.

From one side her managing to defeat Baltic uprisings and Bolshevik movement shows that she has some skills, but from other side I am worried of what kind of madness the ethnic minorities under her rule could experience, as Russians don't have the best record in this case. For this reason, broken up Russia doesn't seem like a bad choice anymore.

As for her politics, her decision to create a bureaucracy is a good one as she understand that it is needed for society to function, and she isn't one of those idiots who yell ''drain the swamp!''. That said, I was left dumbfounded a bit - if she bonks both the monarchists and republicans in head, then who she is? Some other beliefs might be her government's, not her own, but she still is tied to them. Like usual I can agree with a few of them, yet I am rather cordial with bolshies and slavery myself, so...

And M1911 as the personal sidearm? Well, I see that the Mormon's pistol is still going strong. Gotta be honest, I love that pistol as well.
