Russian Security Council

Ataman Stepanov's thoughts on War hound

War hound's children aren't interesting in the slightest, so there is no point to cover them. That said, the War hound itself looks pretty unsettling in one of the images which was provided by intel gatherers - sure, Black Army in Free Territory has a significant power and Free Territory is militarized to the brim as well, but it seems that the realm he rules over seems to function pretty much for purposes of war and looking for new victims to bring terror upon. A good amount of luck seems to be required for any foreign ambassador to deal with Tundra Terra. Besides that, War hound also appears as pretty pompous and grand at times - some of our generals wonder if he is trying way too hard with all of those phrases.

War hound took a keen interest on all things related to strategy, politics, economics... Seems like he is the person who looks largely at the big picture and what role it plays in the life of a society, rather than shorter specializations of it. War hound was certainly forged by fighting wars at odds which would usually bring down a nation, yet what is the most important is what his nation is nowadays. And right now it makes all other nations in his verse' to pale in comparison - that already tells something. As for the ambassadors... War hound seems to have killed over 400 of them - seems like he not only beat Vlad the Impaler in terms of brutality towards ambassadors, but went way over that. Some of the War hound's bright sides are the fact that he has other hobbies than just warfare and slaughter, along with being caring for his own people, but all of that is overshadowed by his dark side; one could also wonder if his nation has any allies at all. In the end, what is the opinion on this War hound? From all the leaders Stepanov has met so far, this particular person has raised the most fear and disgust from Ataman Stepanov. Stepanov understands why War hound and his nation turned out like that, but still doesn't condone the actions. Solid negative.
