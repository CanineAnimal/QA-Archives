Acting President of the United States
[2021-Present]
Report/Opinion:
Mister Grenux Zix. It's a pleasure to meet someone that isn't completely delusional for once.

It seems that first impressions are always the most deceiving ones. If I didn't know any better, I would've assumed that you were just a regular despot like me, but honestly, after I actually take a look at what you stand for, let me just say that I stand corrected and respect the grind. Whatever it is you're trying to accomplish, it reminds me a bit of the states before the State-Unions rose up during Eisenhower's presidency. I am... well, more or less just surprised that someone like you would have been part of the father of decentralization within the AFS. Well, it's good to see you, hopefully we are able to come to a better report on each other than what would be expected. On that note, I would like to note that the portrait is a bit wonky, but it works nonetheless even if it doesn't give the aura of the professional leader of the free systems. Well, at the very least you have a better quality of life than whatever you've been through in the past.

What intrigues me is your most normal background. Leaders usually come from aristocratic families or military generals seizing power, yet here I stand reading the personal details of a fugitive sent to exile by themselves all because of a misunderstanding and a few accidental murders. It's... unique to see a small position like that turn into a de facto figurehead of the free states. From miner to author and eventual freedom fighter against the Andromedan Council's oppression? Well then, if that isn't a life story to make a generation confused then I don't know what exactly can confuse the shit ou of me after that. However, I do congratulate you on that.

I don't have much time so I'll just talk about your politics briefly. Let me summarize it this way; It's this... decentralized system with factionalism popping in. I'd say that I don't like it, but I'm grateful that it was displayed here to be at least somewhat factionalized and probably fractured inside. Just like how it would be in real life. I support you as a person, but ideologically I can't help but adopt a neutral stance. Well, whatever floats your boat.

From the White House, President Townes.
