EDIT as of 12th Sept: And please try your very best not to reserve or call dibs on posting. I know it's not nice as an OP for me to make rules like that, but let's just call this an etiquette.

This is not pointed at anyone in particular, and just a general statement. So see, the idea of getting ninjaed is that while it's a tad annoying, you open up for more opportunities for yourself in order to post and write more. Usually a good thing unless you are just so tight on time.

A dibs or reserving post, personally to me, is a bit jarring. Often times the person happens to be away, and you were left without a reply for 30+ mins, on top of causing more trouble for nations below you especially when you have multiple characters to choose from and you gave no indication of which character to reply to.

So... In short, I'm not like straight out banning it as an OP, but just be very considerate to one another, and embrace being ninjaed, for the lack of a better word. It's not nice when you reserve a post, only to never come back and help someone with equal effort.

Please do carry on, and have a great day! <3
