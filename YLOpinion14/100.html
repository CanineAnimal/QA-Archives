Galerius is, despite his abhorrent political outlook, far too hedonistic and apathetic to his own office to engage in anything resembling an active, motivated campaign of domestic terror. This man, by comparison, appears to have within him a more focused mindset, and a capacity for decisive action - while ordinarily I would laud such a thing in practically any politician, the ends to which he would likely employ it curtail any such sentiments in this particular case. Piety and determination, marred by the hand of delusional, kleptocratic intent, are no more virtues than the cruelty and disregard for one's people the so-called Prince happens to embody. Perhaps the only saving grace in this scenario is the low likelihood of him ever actually ascending to the throne - one may only hope for his demise in battle, so as to wholly safeguard his nation from such a possibility. The very existence of a moron such as this affirms my belief that his dynasty is, by some fundamental, core flaw, naturally disposed to producing mere scum. A shame, for the world he inhabits is a curious one indeed - and yet I find it hard to appreciate any of its wonders when they allow for the existence of a creature such as this. Ah, my final verdict? Was it not clear enough? He is filth. Perhaps not of the lowest order, for believe it or not, there exist things out there more despicable than even he. But filth nonetheless.
