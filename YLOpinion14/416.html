I have said once that “one must become aware when a sake[17] is lost”. My thoughts about this stay the same, but I have no answer for what she must do. I am at a loss for words. Even I do not know what I would do, had I been where she stands. The fayling[18] within me would likely have put a gunstone[19] into my skull at that point.
