“When a leader is a social conservative, such as Herr Pinochet here, and one who values the conservative order of things, I don’t seem to have any reason to withhold commendation for. Of course there are plenty of things to Herr Pinochet than what I have mentioned, and I intend to discuss more of those things in the future...”
- His Imperial Majesty the Emperor of the Imperial Fatherland, Oswald I
