So he is in a charge of an ex-soviet country... In that case, his surprisingly chubby altitude with Russia is certainly an oddity, because based on intel I knew about his timeline's Russia and our timeline's Russian Empire, Russia's neighbors either have a distrust or fear, or are allies which are slowly trying to drift away. Some soldiers stationed in the Rutheno-Ukrainian region heard stories from the locals of just how bastardly the Empire's rule was, even in comparison to the chaos era. Now, to his personal character.

Huh, working on a fish vessel in Arctic seems to be a some sort of popular work for Tarvelians before they can legally work? Got to work only two years on the vessel before the whole big union collapsed, and three years later his conscription letter arrived. This probably gives him some insight about animal world, and perhaps made more environmentalist. Your family is patriotic, sure, but how much of an use the empty patriotism is - I am not so sure. Overall, my altitude is mixed with heavy strokes of ambiguity and not-exactly positiveness.

When it comes to politics, I am going to look purely at his stats this time, and not by what he has enacted or supported in words. Yeah, you are religious and devout to your version of god, alright. His authoritarian side also quite sticks around, and is the bane of opposition in his country. In everything else, he is unfortunately a pitiful moderate, even in conservatism in which he champions himself as an one. Overall, not much else to say about him, other than that he is the typical politician, and would use populism in his favor, yet do something else in office.

And of course, you have to keep a certain personality mask in your office as a leader, even if it isn't your full character in reality. What he said about journalists... Yeah, they need some self-restraint and more, if you know what I mean. As for his laugh, it probably sucks that it comes off as sarcastic, yet is actually his real laugh.
