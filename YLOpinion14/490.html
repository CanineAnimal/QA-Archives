THE PARAMILITARY REGIONS
“ It's been a long, long time since I last did one of these letters, hasn't it, Tolya? You've told me that you've had a far better experience though and that things may not be so bad now. We'll see, we'll see, but don't be surprised if I get another person saying some nonsense about me, or not even bothering to read what you've written on me. So, who is it? Yan Sooyoung? Hang on, shouldn't it be Sooyoung Yan, if we're going by how you Westerners would write this type of names. Apologies to you, Madam - is that how you say it? - Yan, if you receive this letter with your name written on the Western format. New formats made to simplify my job, and all of that.

Now, the dispatch that I've been issued is... not complete, but luckily for me, I've been given some extra information by some of my own militants - spasibo, tovarischi - about who you supposedly are. So, according to what I've seen, you are apparently the so-called "Divine Secretary" - we both know that's false, but I'll get back to that later - of the All-Union of Erudia, the "First Among Equals", but, most above all, the subject of urban legends, because you, supposedly kept appearing on Erudia for many, many times, to the point where according to some deranged nutheads you're the one who began a Third Armaggeddon War? Excuse me, but what the fuck? None of this makes ANY sense. Nothing. Nothing! Interdimensional internment only being freed by Kore- look, let me cut the fluff here, because even Anatoly over here is getting confused.
So, getting that nonsense out of the way, in the long-long time ago, you supposedly were the head of your kind's military, the Generalissimo of Mihnyeo Kind: a patient, utilitarian strategist who... am I reading this shit ri- dropped fucked meteors onto volcanoes and used "nano-weaponry" on food supplies? Excuse me? Dropping something from space? No, no, no, no. No! Cut the bullshit already, would you?!
And yet, you wouldn't last long, as your second-in-command, one genocidal Eirilia Sovngarde, got exiled to the outer periphery, and thus began a civil war amongst your people in which your faction would be defeated, thus, forcing you to be exiled to what I can only assume is Erudia. My condolences. Nobody deserves to have to see their own kind killing themselves in a civil war, not even those who keep calling themselves "divine".

In Erudia - and I have to switch over to the militant-provided intelligence plus other informations on this All-Union - you would arrive in the fortified community of Yangyeol, where the present-day All-Union would take roots, beginning with the publication of your sutras, which would become a... constitution? Excuse me? A-Am I reading this right? You, a random exile from some place practically nobody ever heard of, somehow managed to convince a fuckton of strangers to publish whatever stuff you had written in your exile? And now YOU claim that you taught THEM, in the middle of a nuclear war, to never give up on hope and to be good people? Excuse me, but what the fuck is this Yuri-esque nonsense?! Oh, don't tell me! Next thing you'll be saying is that you established a single-mind Erudia-wide utopia where everyone follows the same ideas, and thus, there is peace throughout the planet you live in! What are you trying to do, be a damned brainwasher?! Do you KNOW what will be happening to the one we have over here on Earth right now? Because, let me tell you this, if you DARE try to copy his deranged rambles about a "single-mind utopia", may the Erudians have no mercy with you AT ALL! Even if their homeland has to be burnt down in order to get rid of you, should you indeed turn out to be a brainwasher! And be thankful that I wouldn't be able to supply them! Be thankful that their revolution wouldn't be able to be supported by a fellow, otherworldly-to-them revolutionary!

But we both know these titles of "Divine Secretary" and "First Among Equals" are false. They're not real, they're only you trying to inflate yourself in the eyes of the entirety of Erudia. If you taught them something, it was to use whatever tools they already had to create an actually functional world. You were and at most still are just another cog in the machine of the All-Union: spinning and working, in order to achieve its common goal, whatever it may be. After all, what's the point of a secretary if there's no superior for them to work for. The masses of Erudia are your superior, and you should keep that in mind. Cut the "Divine Secretary" and "First Among Equals" bullshit. It's only going to impede progress on the achievement of the All-Union's - and thus, your - final goal. Farewell. ”
