And so Sera, the Young Star, fell from the heavens and she fell upon a land devoid of all life. She walked the lands, but found no beauty, but only desert, but only the beginning. Sera asked the spirit of the stars, "O, why have you placed me in these wastes, great spirit?"

And Sera was a good guide, for she danced with the animals as she had danced with the spirits, and she guided the animals as the spirits had taught her.
