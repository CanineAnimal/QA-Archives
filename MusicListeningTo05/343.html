As far as the video itself, this is my understanding:
The video is African themed, and in particular Egyptian. The connection for Damian is that he is a Rasta. Rastas embrace their identity as African and hold Africa in great regard. Africa is where the first humans evolved, the foundations of human civilization arose originally in Africa. Some of the theology of the major Abrahamic religions (Christianity, Islam, Judaism) are said to have been derived from traditional African and Egyptian spirituality. But in modern days, none of this is appreciated, and Africa is exploited. It seems to me that Damian is trying to point out this fact.
How the video's theme connects to Nas...I'm not sure. I don't know enough about him to say.

The musical style is basically a blend of both of their styles: Nas does hip-hop, and Damian does reggae/reggae-fusion. I should also point out that reggae is heavily Rastafari themed and usually expresses messages about Africa and African people, in the land and in the Diaspora.
