The Futurist Movement is an artistic and political movement that focused itself on speed, youth, violence, and industry. It was founded by Filippo Timmaso Marinetti in 1909. The in universe year for the nation is 1926. Factbooks will be created in the coming weeks. I do not subscribe to the tenets of Futurism or Fascism but find the movement to be an extremely interesting look into the development of Italian Fascism and art.
