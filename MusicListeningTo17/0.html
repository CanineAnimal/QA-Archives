XVII


Oh sweet, a music thread.

Over two years since Valentine Z made the last iteration, boy how time flies. Anyways there tons of good music that you all are listening to and plenty to discover from others personal tastes.

I'll grab this from Val's OP about (Cosumar's OP):

Anyways, this is a hangout thread where we post and discuss whatever song/band/album you're currently listening to. It's always cool to sample what fellow NSers are listening to and it's a great way to maybe meet a few who have similar tastes.

Commentary and details about the song you are listening to are encouraged. Please refrain from posting several times in a row if no one else is posting in between. That is considered spam. You can always edit your latest post to add a new song.

Example:
I'm listening to Avantasia's "Mystery of a Blood Red Rose" from Ghostlights (2016). Solid rock opera track.


More rules of this thread:
1) Enjoy the Music!
2) Be Excellent!
3) Follow the first two rule!
