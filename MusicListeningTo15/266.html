by Altmer Dominion » Thu Apr 26, 2018 9:41 pm

King Crimson - Thela Hun Ginjeet

The song actually has an interesting story behind it. King Crimson guitarist/vocalist Adrian Belew was on his way to the studio for a recording session when he got mugged by a couple of ruffians. Instead of just running off, they mistook the demo tape Belew was holding for a recording of the incident, and accused him of being an undercover cop. Belew eventually got out of the situation, but was severely rattled when he met up with the rest of the band. As he was telling the story, Robert Fripp subtly started recording, and used the audio on the resulting studio track. It actually works quite well, and Adrian was a very funny storyteller during live performances (such as here).
