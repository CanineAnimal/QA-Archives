But a leader who is driven by some nebulous scripture, some imagined higher force the effects of which are only palpable in the theoretical afterlife they so fervently yearn for... he may be driven to enact changes that cause detriment to his people in the present, and in the tangible, certain future, in the vague hope that they may aid their attainment of a state of being which may not, in fact, exist at all. I am not saying that the Archduke is one such ruler, for with the information available it really is impossible for me to tell for certain. But for now, I will remain guarded - for a ruler who listens more closely to the words of some ephemeral spirit than those of the people whose lives he controls is little more than a hazard.
