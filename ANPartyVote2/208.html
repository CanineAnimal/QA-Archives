MMP (Mixed-member proportional representation) is an electoral system, in which voters have two votes. One of them is to elect an representative for their electoral district, using FPTP. The second vote is to elect a party. The seats in parliament (House of Commons of Anatoliyanskiy) are then distributed to reflect the result of the party vote and thus achieving proportional representation (PR).
