In Arvenia, what Parliamentary and Extra-Parliamentary party would I vote for?


Parliamentary
Alright, I believe I have got two options here, not going to lie...
So, the first ones are:

------------The Royal Assistance Party
Now then, these guys... Let me try and make my thoughts brief for now.
So, I really don't care about monarchy, but I do care about what the RAP upholds in their protection of the environment, regulation of economy and social responsibility against the top 1%... I am sure they are also quite a bit more. Into authority compared to the Arvenian Value Front and the Arvenian Self-Defense Party, which does mean less BS when it comes to the ideas of tackling crime and other wrongdoings that would harm a country and it's peoples...
The only potential deal breaker is in their religious policy... I am okay with keeping a state religion, however if they end up attempting to force Megandelanism down the common people's throat, then that's a nope from me...

------------Arvenian Self-Defense Party
Okay, the National Conservatives... So, I support their neutral, pacifistic stance in terms of international affairs, to an extent of course... If they wish to turn Arvenia into a hermit nation similar to the DPRK in that sense, then NOPE... I appreciate their focus on crime control, and any party that supports the homeless... Well, getting homes is a plus.
Only problem is that conscription, and it is a huge problem to me... If they don't want to get rid of that, I'd probably be staying away from them...

Final Verdict? :: The Royal Assistance Party


Extra-Parliamentary
Okay, well... I've got two serious entries (I guess?) and one whacky one...
There isn't much to unpack here, so I get to be brief...

------------The Green Market Party
I do not really know, but there is... Something somewhat appealing about these guys, combining National Conservatism and Green Politics... They want to save the world from climate change AND take a hard stance against crime and anarchists? Sign me up honestly!

------------Small Business Party
I believe that this is something I could support, I guess? I appreciate the protection of the small guys against the big guys, but considering everything, there isn't too much to convince me, I guess...

------------ThE MeNtAl HeAlTh PaRtY
Free the oppressed crazy people! Every drug imaginable legalized! Make Arvenia a nuthouse! This is the dream!!!

Final Verdict? :: The Green Market Party
