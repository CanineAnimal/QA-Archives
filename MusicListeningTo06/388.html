I had major homesickness issues when I was a little kid, so these lyrics make me kinda smile.

I also had pretty major general emotional responses to a lot of things. I'd hit myself hard with something (Like a damn vacuum cleaner handle in the head), and I would be fine, but a little pained, but as soon as people started asking me if I was okay, I'd say I was fine but for some reason them asking if I was fine would make me cry. I don't know how many times I cried for no reason. I cried before even going ANYWHERE when I was homesick once. Hell, I remember once my aunt take me back home because of me being so homesick, and another time we were on the other side of South Lake Tahoe and my dad came and got me.

So glad those pretty much passed over. It was a bit concerning, looking back at that. Now I can be at grandparents for a month and I still don't feel that homesick.

Now I just get emotional easy in other ways but whatever. Still not great in that regard.
