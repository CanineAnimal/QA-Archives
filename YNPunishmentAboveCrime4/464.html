Since AN haven't give a new crime, I will address to AAN first.

Vandalism of public-owned property or perceived public-accessed property (e.g. a private-owned statue fountain in cemetery that is usually seen by public and have became a scenery in town) will be sentenced with repayment to the damage of the property that was given by independent source, fines 0.5 to 5 times of the damage and potential incarceration up to 6 months if the damage is irreparable or the court finds the offender have serious and unforgivable intentions.

Vandalism to private property is considered as "Antragsdelikt" (No trial without Complaint), unless done with other crime (Intimidation, Extortion, Burglary, Arson, Intention to Injure or Murder, etc.). The offender will be sentenced to same punishment listed above, with the fines goes directly to reparation to the victim or the property owner.
