by Scytharum » Tue Jun 14, 2022 3:44 pm

Mass Murder - A life sentence, consisting of, on average, 100 years, as well as an estimate of each person's remaining lifespan(by a metric of an eighty-year lifespan, for example if it was five 70-year old victims the sentence would be 100 years plus an additional 50) is almost guaranteed, as well as the individual's assets either being frozen or, more likely, sold off. In some Scythaic Commonwealths, the death penalty is issued instead of a life sentence. This punishment is issued to all individuals actively involved, whether one individual or entire cults.

Mass Murder of an Ethnic/Religious Minority - Same as above, except the individual's assets are sold off to surviving members of that ethnic/religious minority

Stealing Government Weapons - Depends on the weapon stolen, as there are four classes of this crime. Class 1 weapons are generally accessible, and are treated as standard cases of theft, in which the asset is returned to its owner and the thief is required to pay an equivalent amount to that owner. Class 2 weapons are exclusively used by Alphamarines, the elite branch of the Scythaic military, and theft of these weapons will result in a sentence of at least 20 years, as well as 10 additional for each weapon stolen. Class 3 weapons are WMDs or Starfleet vessels, and the theft of these has been determined to be either a life sentence or the death penalty, partially depending on the judge's sentence, though this is an almost guaranteed death penalty. Class 4 weapons are SCP Foundation weapons in Scythaic holdings, and theft of these weapons is punishable by immediate and total termination of both the vagrant and any observers outside the Foundation or UCS government, in order to maintain the Foundation's secrecy.

Arson - Depends on severity. Approximate value of the assets destroyed by the individual are taken from the individual to reimburse the business. If the assets destroyed exceeds the arsonist's net value, then the reimbursement will be pinned upon them as a debt to be paid. As well, if any individuals die in such events, the arsonist in question is given 5 years in prison for each person killed, and the costs for the dead victims' funerals will be directly confiscated from an individual's personal assets.

Torture - Again, depends on severity. Medical payments of the individual to be nursed back to health are paid by the torturer, and the torturer is to be imprisoned for at least 5 years, often more depending on a judge's ruling.

Threatening to use WMDs: See Class 3 Weapon Punishment.

Escape from High Security Prison: Individual will be sent back to said facility, and the time they spent away will be added onto their sentence, i.e. an individual who escaped for a year would have a year added onto their sentence.

Attacking a Government Building: Damages will be reimbursed by the attacker, and the attacker will likely receive at least a life sentence. If this facility is involved with the SCP Foundation, a death penalty is guaranteed.

In short, a lot of punishments for crimes in Scytharum revolve around economic reimbursement of the affected, as a disincentive towards lawbreaking.
