Well krautrock more specifically refers to music the experimental scene in Germany during the late 60's. There's some similar concepts that carry over from bands - long jam/improv sessions, electronic presence and for some bands the Motorik beat but they vary pretty wildly. You get stuff like early Can and German Oak which is just pretty jam heavy, slighty noise rock music, to Faust's 2deep4u first album and Der Bluthsarch's which is borderline poppy avant garde music crossed with slightly terrifying ambient and violent noise.

really good genre that i've yet to mine thoroughly

i wouldn't call someone out for labeling something new as krautrock as, while there's a great amount of distinction between artists, there's also a definite krautrock flavour.
