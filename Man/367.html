Baiddsound wrote:Not a prompt, but can someone please explain how stands work? I haven't watched the show

A「Stand」is basically an entity physically manifested through a person's psyche; people who use stands are called a「Stand User」. Stands can not be seen by people without stand powers BUT Stands can interact with regular people. The only way to see and defend yourself against an enemy Stand is to have a Stand of your own. A Stand user usually shares their consciousness with their Stand, is able to see through its eyes, and suffers an equivalent injury if the Stand is damaged. (If an enemy stand cuts off your stand's finger, your own finger is coming off as well) but the same is true for the enemy stand user, too.

One final note (so as to not drown you in Jojo info): For our purposes, there are two main types of stands: Short-range and long-range stands. Short-range stands usually have much higher strength and power but can't attack beyond somewhere around 2 meters while long-range stands are usually weaker but are able to operate with the user being a safe distance away.
